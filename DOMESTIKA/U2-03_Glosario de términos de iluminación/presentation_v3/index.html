The Fundamentals

Light

Radiant energy (380-770nm) capable of producing visual sensation.

Natural Light

Generated naturally, primarily by the Sun.

Artificial Light

Man-made sources: lamps, street lights, strobes.

Bioluminescence

Light created by living organisms.

Physics

Light Interactions

  • Reflection: Ability of surfaces to bounce light (Reflectance).
  • Absorption: Ability to neither reflect nor transmit light.
  • Transmission: Ability of materials to allow light passage (Transmittance).

Philosophy

Lighting Qualities

Ambient Lighting: General illumination throughout an area.

Focal Glow: The pool of light that draws attention and separates the important.

Play of Brilliants: Dynamic light that creates interest and stimulates the spirit.
