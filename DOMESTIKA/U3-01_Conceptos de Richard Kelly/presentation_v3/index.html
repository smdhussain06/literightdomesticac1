The Triad

Core Concepts

  • Ambient Luminescence: The light that provides general visibility. Background light.
  • Focal Glow: The pool of light that directs the eye. Task light.
  • Play of Brilliants: Dynamic light that creates interest and stimulates the spirit. Decorative light.
