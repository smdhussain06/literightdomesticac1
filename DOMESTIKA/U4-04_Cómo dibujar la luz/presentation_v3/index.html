Drafting

Installation Logic

Drawing in cross-section is essential to communicate how fixtures are integrated into the architecture.

  • Recessed: Embedded in the ceiling/slab for a seamless look.
  • Superimposed: Surface mounted for easy access and retrofitting.
  • Suspended: Hanging fixtures for volume and decorative impact.

Projection

The Beam of Light

Visualizing the photometric footprint of the luminaire.

  • Narrow Beams: Dramatic focal glow, high contrast.
  • Wide Beams: Uniform ambient luminescence, broad coverage.
  • Directionality: Indicating the tilt and aim to assist on-site installation.
