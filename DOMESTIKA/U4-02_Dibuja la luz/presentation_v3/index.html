Strategy

Lighting Hierarchy

  1. Desk Area: Cold temperature for concentration.
  2. Vanity Table: Cold temperature for accurate grooming/makeup.
  3. Closet: Warm temperature with high CRI (Museum style) to distinguish garment colors.
