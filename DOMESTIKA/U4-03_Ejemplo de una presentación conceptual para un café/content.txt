      General Conditions

      ✓ The lighting in the patios and retail space, such as the main office, should be warm, with a maximum of 300 K.



      ✓ The lighting equipment in simple high spaces should be recessed (A) and must be hidden in the ceiling.



      ✓ For double and triple height spaces, the lighting equipment should be suspended (B).



      ✓ Transition spaces (stairs/hallways) will have graze lighting, with the light in the floor (C).



      ✓ In open spaces, patios, and/or terraces, we’ll support the lighting in the transition spaces with wall-mounted lights.



      ✓ For the wall lighting, we propose two kinds.
                 Indirect: Lighting towards the wall (to reveal the texture) (D)
                 Direct: Useful lighting, to determine spaces, set tables. (E)


      ✓ The lighting in the kitchen must be appropriate for the work done there. We’ll install 4000 k light fixtures with a neutral light.
          These light fixtures must have a low glare index.
