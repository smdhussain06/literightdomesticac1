Philosophy

The Message

Our goal is to always support the message of the interior architecture.

  • Highlight the predominant texture (Chukum).
  • Reveal warmth and structural rhythm.
  • Create moments of "brilliant surprises" throughout the space.
02
LiteRight Academy

Standards

Technical Guidelines

Temperature: Warm tones (max 3000K) for public spaces; Neutral (4000K) for the kitchen.

Integration:

  • Recessed (Hidden) fixtures for single-height spaces.
  • Suspended decorative lights for double/triple heights.
  • Graze lighting for transition areas (stairs/hallways).
