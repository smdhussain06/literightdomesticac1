Lesson 1.1 — Who is LITELAB? (10 mins)

LITELAB is an independent lighting design consultancy specializing in sustainable lighting, home automation, and IoT solutions for global environments. Our journey began with a passion for design excellence and a commitment to human-centric lighting.

Global Footprint

  • 2003: Foundation in Milan, Italy.
  • 2014–2023: Expansion across India (Chennai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad).
  • 2023–2024: New hubs in Dubai (UAE) and Colombo (Sri Lanka).
💡 Our Mission: To deliver qualitative, innovative solutions by staying at the pulse of global trends while prioritizing client satisfaction and environmental sustainability.
