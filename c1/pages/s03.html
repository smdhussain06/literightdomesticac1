Video 1.3 Roadmap & Methodology

SCENE 1The 6-Step Journey0:00–0:45
[VISUAL: Animated timeline showing Stages 1 through 6]

Every successful lighting project follows a clear path. At LITELAB, we've refined this path into six essential stages. This roadmap will guide you through the entire course, providing you with a transparent and world-class workflow used in our global projects.

SCENE 2Design Development (Stages 1-3)0:45–1:45
[VISUAL: Sketches, Renders, and Detailed Technical Tables]

We begin with Concept Design—where we analyze the site and establish the mood. Then we move into Schematic Design, creating layouts and full renders. In Detailed Design, we finalize the technical package, including control layouts and full BOQs with specifications.

SCENE 3Execution (Stages 4-6)1:45–2:30
[VISUAL: Tender documents, site photos with wiring, and final project handover photos]

Next is Tender Design—where we validate vendor compliance. During Installation, we oversee wiring, mock-ups, and site inspections. Finally, in Project Handover, we provide as-built drawings and soft copies, ensuring a seamless transition for the client.

SCENE 4Final Project & Vision2:30–3:00
[VISUAL: "Designed for Happiness" title card]

Throughout this course, you will apply these steps to your own project. Remember: our goal is to design for happiness. By the end of this journey, you'll be able to manage any lighting project with the same professional standards we use at LITELAB. Let's begin the technical journey in the next unit.
