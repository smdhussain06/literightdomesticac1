SCENE 2Technologies0:30–3:30

Incandescent — heated filament, warm, 90% energy becomes heat. Halogen — improved incandescent, 25% more efficient. Fluorescent — UV converted to visible light, efficient. LED — semiconductor, dominant technology, 25,000–50,000 hours. OLED — thin-film panels, soft diffuse light, emerging tech.

The lamp/module (light source), housing (enclosure + heat management), optics (lenses, reflectors, diffusers that shape the beam), driver (converts voltage for LEDs, enables dimming), and mounting system (recessed, surface, suspended, track, free-standing).
