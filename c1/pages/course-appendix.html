📖 Appendix & Glossary

Quick Reference Glossary

TermDefinition
Ambient luminescenceGeneral, uniform background illumination
Beam angleThe cone of light emitted by a directional fixture
Candela (cd)Unit of luminous intensity in a direction
CCTCorrelated Color Temperature — Kelvin scale
CRIColor Rendering Index — accuracy of color appearance
Focal glowDirected, concentrated light on a specific area
GlareExcessive brightness causing discomfort
GrazingLight aimed parallel to surface to reveal texture
IP RatingIngress Protection — resistance to dust/water
IK RatingImpact protection for enclosures
LEDLight Emitting Diode
Lumen (lm)Unit of total light output
LuminaireComplete light fixture (housing + lamp + optics)
LuminanceBrightness as perceived by the eye (cd/m²)
Lux (lx)Unit of illuminance (1 lumen per m²)
OLEDOrganic LED (thin-film, diffuse panel)
Play of brilliantsSparkle and decorative points of light
UGRUnified Glare Rating
Wall-washingUniform illumination of a wall surface
Watts (W)Unit of electric power consumption
