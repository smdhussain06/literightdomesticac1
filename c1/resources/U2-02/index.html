The Materials
in the Space
Black-out curtains.
Plasticized fabric, 75% polyester and 25%
fiberglass. It prevents light from coming
in from the exterior and ambient light
needs to be turned on. However, since
it's white, it reflects light.
Vinyl paint wall finish.
The color helps prevent light from
reflecting in the space during the day. But
if it’s a cloudy day, it won’t be useful since
the ceiling light will need to be turned on.
