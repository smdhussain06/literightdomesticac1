1. Desk area
This is located in front of a window. During
the day extra artificial lighting isn't necessary.
After 5 or 6 p.m., the ceiling light should be
turned on. Since it’s a work area, the light
temperature should be cold.
2. Vanity table area
Lighting needs to be installed around the
mirror and aimed at the user so they can do
their hair and makeup. The ideal light
temperature
is cold.
3. Closet area and upper-level organization
The user indicated that this space has the
least amount of light. It’s a problem because
in the mornings they can’t distinguish well
between colors when deciding what to wear.
In addition, the upper area has a big storage
space with purses, which requires a museum
exhibition style of lighting. The suggested
light temperature is warm.

Client Profile

–
User in
the Space

General area of the space:
Since it’s a bedroom that was adapted to also be
a work space, the current ceiling light doesn't
light the whole space well enough. The ideal
temperature to use is warm, not cold like the
current temperature.
Decorative table lamp and candles:
The user decided to keep them where they
currently are. Since they’re just for decoration
and/or reading at night, they're in reach of the
bed so they can be turned off easily.
