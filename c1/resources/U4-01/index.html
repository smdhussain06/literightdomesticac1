Functionality
–
Does it do its job?
Is it appropriate for the activities
being done?
Which other light fixture would
you use?
Decorative light fixtures are only
good as ambient lighting. They work
really well for working after 5 p.m. in
front of the computer. However,
when carrying out more detailed
activities, it isn't useful.
No, you'll need a light fixture that’s
closer to the work table for
activities that require more light.
I would add a light fixture to the
desk that can be aimed in any
direction you need. It could be a
clip-on light so it occupies as little
space as possible. The temperature
definitely needs to be cold.
